Featured Work

Projects

📱
Mobile App · Islamic
Qaf — Quran Memorization App

Qaf is a mobile application designed to help users memorize the Quran in a structured and personalized way. Many people struggle to stay consistent with memorization due to the lack of a clear plan and difficulty tracking their progress.

The app solves this by creating a personalized memorization plan automatically, based on user preferences — the surah or juz to memorize, the number of verses or pages per day, and available free days. It also helps users track daily progress and ensures they don't lose track of what they have already memorized by organizing their schedule and adjusting the plan when needed.
